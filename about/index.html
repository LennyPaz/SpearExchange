About The Spear Exchange

The Spear Exchange is a student-focused marketplace built to make campus buying, selling, and short-term housing discovery feel more trustworthy, more organized, and easier to use.

Our Mission

We are building a trusted and affordable marketplace for Florida State University students, designed to strengthen campus connections while making everyday items, creative products, and sublease opportunities easier to find and navigate.

Trust first The experience should feel more reliable and transparent than a scattered mix of student group posts.
Faster decisions Listings, messages, and sublease details should help students understand what matters without extra friction.
Built for Seminoles The platform is intentionally shaped around the practical needs of FSU students living, studying, and trading on campus.

What We’re Building

The Spear Exchange is evolving into a single student marketplace where regular items, 3D printed FSU-inspired products, and subleases all live inside one cleaner flow. The goal is not just to list things, but to make campus commerce easier to browse, compare, and act on.
